About this site

The Science Injury Tracker is a database of user-submitted incident reports of injuries and difficulties faced by research scientists while undertaking field-based research. Inspired by a recent article in Nature, the Tracker is a vital resource for researchers in the design phase of their experimental studies, aggregating data on potential hazards within geographical areas of interest so as to ensure better preparedness for future researchers.
